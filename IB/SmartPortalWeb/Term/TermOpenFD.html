The structure of Personal Account

1.Fixed Deposit Receiving the Interest by Month or Maturity

One product of Siam Commercial Bank given to its customers is Fixed Deposit that the customer can receive the interest month by month. It has low risk, but high interest rate and it is good for financial planning for the future use. If you have a continuous revenue from your investment and do not require to spend the money, this fixed term deposit is the answer. Siam Commercial Bank also offers a Fixed Deposit on Maturity. In this product customer can avail benefit of Fixed Deposit after the tenure of Fixed Deposit. .

2. Benefits of Account

  • Receive the interest by the end of every month.
  • Receive the interest of foreign currency at the highest rate per year.
  • You can choose the time frame for your deposit (1, 2 or 3 years).
  • This account can be used as a collateral with Siam Commercial Bank to request for a loan or credit card.

3.Type of currency for deposit

  • Lao KIP.
  • Thai Baht.
  • US Dollar.

7. How to open the account

  • Access the Phongsavanh Internet Banking through your desktop or mobile.
  • The Link to access Siam Commercial Bank Internet Banking is https://www.bp.com.la
  • Be careful about the link and do not provide your User ID and Password at any other links or webpages.
  • Maintain at least LAK1,000,000, THB4,000 or USD100 in your savings account.
  • Login to your Internet banking using your User ID and Password.
  • Select Term Deposit link from left hand side menu.
  • Click on Add New Deposit.
  • Fill in the details and select the Maturity Type and Tenure of Fixed Deposit.
  • Agree to the Terms and Conditions of Fixed Deposit and Click Continue.
  • Review all the details and submit the request to create an Fixed Deposit online.
  • You will receive a Success message on the screen when a New Fixed Deposit is created.

8.Sync Fixed Deposits

  • Customer can Sync the Fixed Deposits opened through branches in the internet banking by clicking the Sync Accounts option in the Internet Banking.
  • Customer can close a Fixed Deposit opened through internet banking via branches. This fixed deposit closed through branches can be synced in Internet banking.
  • Customer can close a Fixed Deposit opened through branch via Internet Banking. To see the fixed deposit in the internet banking customer can use the Sync Accounts option.
